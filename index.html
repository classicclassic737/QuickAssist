üåü What is Quick Assist?

Quick Assist is Microsoft's official remote assistance tool that allows users to give and receive remote support over the internet. Built directly into Windows 10 and Windows 11, it enables IT professionals, help desk technicians, and everyday users to troubleshoot problems and provide technical support without installing third-party software.
